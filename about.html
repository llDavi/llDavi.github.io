My Philosophy

I believe that true security comes from understanding how systems can be broken. By thinking like an attacker, we can build more resilient defenses and anticipate threats before they materialize.

My approach combines rigorous technical analysis with practical insights, ensuring that research translates into actionable security improvements. Every vulnerability discovered is an opportunity to strengthen the ecosystem.
